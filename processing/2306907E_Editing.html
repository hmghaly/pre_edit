The Security Council took note of the letter from the Foreign Minister of Colombia dated 14 February 2023 (S/2023/112) in which he updated the Council on the progress made by the Government of Colombia in its talks with various groups and in which he requested the expansion of the mandate of the United Nations Verification Mission to support these efforts, in particular in the verification of ceasefires.
